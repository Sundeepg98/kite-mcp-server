# Example use cases

## Portfolio review

**Ask Claude:**
Could you analyse my current portfolio holdings with the following details:
- Top 5 positions by market value
- Best and worst performers
- Sector distribution
- Overall portfolio beta and volatility
- Unrealised P&L summary

Also plot my portfolio curve against an equal-weight composite index of **Nifty500**, **Gold**, and **Nifty 10 year G-Sec index**.
