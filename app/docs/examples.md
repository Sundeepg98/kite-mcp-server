# Example Use Cases

Here are some powerful ways to use Kite MCP with your AI assistant.

## Portfolio Review

**Ask:**

> Could you analyze my current portfolio holdings with the following details:
> - Top 5 positions by market value
> - Best and worst performers
> - Sector distribution
> - Overall portfolio beta and volatility
> - Unrealized P&L summary
>
> Also plot my portfolio curve against an equal-weight composite index of Nifty500, Gold, and Nifty 10 year G-Sec index.

![Portfolio Analysis](/static/img/first.png)

## Analyzing F&O Positions

## Market Overview

**Ask:**

> Please provide a summary of today's market conditions including:
> - Major index movements
> - Sector performance
> - Any notable news affecting my holdings
> - Stocks with unusual volume or price action

## More Ideas

Try asking your AI assistant:

- "Set up a GTT order to sell RELIANCE if it drops below 2400"
- "What's my margin available for trading?"
- "Show me my order history for today"
- "Create a price alert for TCS at 3500"
- "Calculate my portfolio's exposure to IT sector"
- "What would be my P&L if NIFTY moves 2% down?"
