## Business model

- Self-hosted option: users run their own copy — zero cost to us, zero revenue from them

- No advertising, no data sales, no commissions from Zerodha or any other party

## Why this matters for an incident-response audience

A regulator or compliance team reading this will want to assess proportionality. Relevant context:

1. **No profit motive to keep running.** If asked to shut down, no revenue stream collapses.
2. **No user harm from pricing.** We do not charge, so users have no sunk cost or lock-in.
3. **Modest scale = modest regulatory footprint.** We are not an "entity" in the commercial sense — this is an open-source project with a free hosted demo.
4. **Kite Connect fee paid to Zerodha** demonstrates good-faith direct business relationship with the broker (not circumventing their commercial model).
